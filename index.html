Meri Pyaari Bindu: 2017

Meri Pyaari Bindu ( transl. My Dear Bindu) is a 2017 Indian Hindi-language romantic comedy film, written by Suprotim Sengupta and directed by Akshay Roy. It features Parineeti Chopra and Ayushmann Khurrana in the lead roles.

Producer: Aditya Chopra, Maneesh Sharma
